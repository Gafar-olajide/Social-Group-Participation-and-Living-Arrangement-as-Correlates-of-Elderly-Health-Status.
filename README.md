# 📊 Social Group Participation and Living Arrangement as Correlates of Elderly Health Status.

## 🔍 Project Overview:
This project analyses how social group participation and living arrangements influence the health status of elderly individuals. The goal is to identify key social and demographic factors associated with health outcomes.

______

## 🎯 Objectives
- Examine the effect of social group participation on elderly health 
- Assess the impact of living arrangement on health status 
- Analyze the combined effect of both variables 

______

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
- STATA (Statistical Analysis)
- SPSS (Descriptive & Inferential Analysis)

______

## 📈 Methodology
- Quantitative and qualitative analysis
- Data cleaning and preprocessing in Excel  
- Descriptive statistics to summarize variables  
- Inferential analysis (e.g., chi-square, regression) using STATA & SPSS  

______

## 🔑 Key Insights
- Social participation shows a significant association with better health outcomes  
- Elderly individuals living with others tend to have improved health status  
- Living arrangement and social engagement are key predictors of health
